1. BASIC Operation

Drag a point on the image by your pointing device such as mouse, then a line and a circle appear and become extended. The line and the circle mean vector and range of a wobble, respectively. You can stop dragging at an arbitrarily point.

You can change the shape of the circle by dragging the border of that. You can also move it by dragging the center of that.

The controls on the downside allow you change parameters such like as velocity and largeness.

2. Changing the image

Drag and drop a image file (JPEG/PNG/GIF) on the image to change that. you can also do it by the file-open dialog or copy & paste from other software.

3. Benchmark

Check the "benchmark" check-box to show frame-rate. notice that both the image size and Java version could affect the frame-rate.
